Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

1756 - 1791

Who was Mozart

  • Born in middle 18th Century, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (also famously known as 'Mozart') was one of the most influential, popular and prolific composers of the classical period.
  • He composed over 600 works, including some of the most famous and loved pieces of symphonic, chamber, operatic, and choral music.
  • Mozart was born in Salzburg, Austria to a musical family. He had a sister along whom he played music in his early times.

Why was Mozart great and Still Is

Mozart is considered great for his astonishing breadth and depth of composition, mastering nearly every classical music form and elevating them to new heights with unparalleled melodic beauty, structural clarity, and emotional depth. He was a musical prodigy, composing over 600 works in just 30 years and creating some of the most perfect and enduring masterpieces in history, known for their sparkling textures and powerful expressions of human emotion.

The level and the musical prowess that Mozart had was unbelievable and no composer after him composed at his level. Everyone loved his music as it was so beatiful and high class. Every classical composer and the later composers praised Mozart's musical skills.

Impact of Mozart's work

Mozart's work impacted society by shaping the classical music landscape through innovation in form and harmony, influencing future composers like Beethoven, and setting new standards for opera with realistic and complex character portrayals.
